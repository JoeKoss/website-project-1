The Implications of Purple

     Purple is an interesting color that is a mix of red and blue. Red is a warm color and blue is a cool color, but purple is a cool color ("Basic color schemes - Introduction to Color Theory").

     The word purple has a deep history, transitioning from Greek to Latin, Latin to Old English, and finally ending up in the form that is used today ("Intro to the purples"). Purple has had connections to royalty since the Byzantine Empire, causing it to represent power and wealth ("Intro to the purples").

     In some European nations, including the U.K., Italy, Brazil, and Thailand, purple signifies death and grieving ("SmarterTravel").In the United States, purple is a symbol of honor and courage ("SmarterTravel"). Because of these varying connotations, purple is perhaps not the best choice when creating content for a world-wide audience.
