Describing Teal

     Being a mix between colors, it is difficult to determine any concrete cultural significance for the color Teal. Teal is a cold color because it is a mix of color that falls blue and green on the color wheel ("Basic color schemes - Introduction to Color Theory").

     It is a great color for an individual who has just finished a difficult or stressful day, as it combines the best psychological aspects of both blue and green. Blue elicits feelings of tranquility and calm while green elicits feelings of balance and peace (Sheppard).

     Teal is a great color to apply to marketing or web design, as teal can also evoke feelings of trustworthiness and reliability ("The Color Turquoise"). It is also relatively easy to view in its darker variants, making it a good pick as a complementary color for a page banner.
